IMAGE PLACEHOLDERS
==================

Replace these files with real images before launch.

hero.jpg
  Purpose:  Hero section background or split image
  Ratio:    4:5 portrait (recommended 800×1000px or 900×1125px)
  Direction: Woman in a calm, reflective setting — writing, thinking,
             in a warm interior space. Muted, editorial tones.

founder.jpg
  Purpose:  Michelle Mah founder portrait
  Ratio:    3:4 portrait (recommended 600×800px)
  Direction: Professional but warm. Natural light preferred.
             Not a corporate headshot — something grounded and human.

post-1.jpg through post-6.jpg
  Purpose:  Article card thumbnails
  Ratio:    16:9 landscape (recommended 800×450px)
  Direction: Editorial, quiet confidence. Women in workspaces,
             reading, coaching settings, movement, quiet reflection.
             Avoid cheesy stock poses.

about-image.jpg
  Purpose:  About page image
  Ratio:    4:5 portrait or 1:1 square

Image treatment notes:
  - Prefer warm, muted tones over bright/saturated
  - Keep backgrounds soft — cream, sage, natural wood, linen
  - Avoid heavy filters or over-processed looks
