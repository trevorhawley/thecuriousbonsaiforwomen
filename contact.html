Get in touch

We'd like to hear from you

Whether you're exploring coaching, interested in future workshops, have a question about the content here, or just want to make contact — you're welcome to reach out.

Replies are personal and thoughtful. Please allow a few days — we don't use automated responses.

A note before you write

This is a real conversation, not a funnel

If you reach out, a person reads your message. We're not going to add you to a list or send you a sequence of automated emails. If something you've read here has stayed with you and you want to explore it further — whether that's through coaching, or just a conversation — this is the right place to start.

For direct coaching enquiries

The Curious Bonsai

For formal coaching or therapy engagements — including one-on-one work, corporate programmes, and speaking — you can also reach out directly through The Curious Bonsai's contact page.

What to expect

Good faith replies

We respond to every genuine message. If you're exploring whether coaching or therapy is the right next step, it's fine to say so — you don't need to arrive with certainty. If you're interested in a workshop that hasn't launched yet, let us know and we'll keep you informed when it does.
